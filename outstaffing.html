ABOUT OUTSTAFFING

Qualified BI specialists on an hourly basis for development projects of any complexity. Our outstaffing service provides qualified BI specialists on an hourly basis. The project can be managed by the customer or by an Data Integrity Services project manager.

  • Involving Data Integrity Services qualified specialists costs less than maintaining in-house staff
  • You are free to stop receiving services at any time. It’s much more effort-consuming to lay off employees
  • You are free from additional tax, sick leave and insurance expenses, and pay for time worked only
  • Reliable IT specialists are at your disposal for a transparent and predictable monthly fee
  • Skills of Data Integrity Services employees are fully consistent with their declared qualifications and guaranteed by the company reputation and experience
